Explore. Encounter. Endeavour

Author: Karishma

Date: Nov 1, 2019

Do you find yourself wondering about the endless possibilities of untapped potential? Do you find yourself hooked to Shark Tank, boasting how you could have pitched better? Does your mind wander to innovation in invention? Or do you simply wield the ultimate weapon of curiosity?

IIITD’s Entrepreneurship Cell is here to help facilitate your dreams, further your vision and foster the true unwavering spirit of entrepreneurship. The club is reinstating its vision and purpose with revamped efforts. A sanctuary for the dreamers, learners and leaders.

ECell has graciously curated an array of events for the academic year. Each event designed to embolden the embers of entrepreneurship. Varied formats and interactive sessions will be held to ensure that a platform is available for students to indulge in endeavours with endless imagination and enthusiasm. Events like PitchCafé will be organised where the participants can present their ideas. Other events like talks, workshops and competitions will be organised to build a community of thinkers here at IIITD.

To kick off the year we have devised an activity intended to fuel a critically thinking mind. This induction period activity is carefully constructed as a group activity where the students have to walk through the entrepreneurial process from problem analysis, relevant research, idea conception, all the way through till presentation. This is meant to be a contained exposure for them. This will serve not only as a crucial team building, time management project but will also provide a glimpse at the exciting, whirling world of entrepreneurship.

We aim to develop deep into different domains, brainstorm, innovate and ultimately create. A process designed to equip the individual to take on challenges and emerge victorious.
