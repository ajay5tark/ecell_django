E-SUMMIT 20'
20th - 22nd March
IIIT Delhi

What's all the hype about?

Immerse yourself in two days of entrepreneurial extravaganza! With an unprecedented amount of creativity, countless inspiring ideas, a confluence of entrepreneurial fanatics and a gamut of activities, the very first edition of E-Summit’2020 is here to celebrate and empower entrepreneurship amongst everyone, with the aim to bring together startup enthusiasts, new-age entrepreneurs, young ideators and venture capitalists on a single platform. With two days of jam-packed events and frenzy amongst all, be ready to encounter a plethora of opportunities coming your way!

Events

Productathon

Productathon is a 24 hour hackathon where enthusiasts across various domains collaborate to find solution to a provided problem statement.

The Godfather

People participate for the thrill of taking over the underworld and emerge as the godfather and putting their grey cells to work to devise a winning strategy.

Wolf of the Wall Street

In life, you don’t get what you deserve. You get what you negotiate. Our event aims to create a business simulation that tests these very negotiation skills.

Stock - Pitch

A single page document for giving a recommendation to people about whether they should buy or sell a particular stock.

Stock Market Auction

Investment is an art which has become a necessity in the current world as the opportunity cost of money left idle keeps on increasing. This event gives participants a glimpse of the daily life of stock investors.

Pitch Cafe 3.0

Different teams pitch their ideas in several rounds which are later judged and some of them are rewarded accordingly.

IPL Auction
