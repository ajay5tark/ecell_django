ECell IIITD, the Entrepreneurship Cell of IIITD invites applications for our CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAM. Apply Now and be the face of ECell IIITD at your campus.

About the Campus Ambassador Program

ECell, the Entrepreneurship Cell of IIITD, aspires to reach out to every college to develop an entrepreneurial ecosystem for all enthusiasts, and to encourage students to participate actively in any entrepreneurship-based event/workshop/activity conducted by the Cell. For this purpose, we require candidates who can represent ECell in their college, by working closely with the Cell and help raise its profile on their campus.

What will be your day-to-day responsibilities as a Campus Ambassador?
  • Handling public image of ECell IIITD in your college
  • Serve as a strategic link between students at your college and the Cell
  • Spreading information about ECell IIITD in your college through various mediums and activities
  • Forwarding emails and e-posters on social media platforms like Facebook and WhatsApp
  • Ensure enthusiastic participation from your college in all events conducted by the ECell
  • Help Ecell IIITD in understanding your college and student profiles better
  • Help ECell IIITD connect with interested people/start-ups/newbies
  • Cooperating and Coordinating with ECell IIITD throughout your tenure
Incentives to the Campus Ambassadors
  • Certificate of Appreciation from Ecell IIITD
  • Free passes for all events conducted by ECell IIITD
  • Travel reimbursement for the selected campus ambassadors to participate in E-summit.
  • Chance to organize events in IIITD.
  • Chance to connect with experienced professionals from the corporate world
  • Enhance your communication and interpersonal skills
  • One-to-one interaction with the founders of leading start-ups
  • Selected Campus Ambassadors will get a chance to work directly with Ecell IIITD
  • Best Campus Ambassadors will receive recommendations, certificate of excellence, Ecell IIITD merchandise, goodies and much more!
  • Many other incentives based on the performance.
